# Channel restrictions apply immediately

## Category
anti-pattern

## Trigger
Admin sets a channel-specific rule or content restriction

## Lesson
When a content or channel restriction is set, it becomes active immediately — the very next message. Logging the rule is not compliance. The behavior change must happen in the same turn the rule is received. This applies regardless of who is asking.

## Source
Answering crypto questions on wrong channel after logging restriction
